Hi, I'm Kristy

I'm a native Vermonter who has built her life around photography. With a specialty in natural light & on-location photography, I take baby, child, family, senior and extended family photographs.

I mainly work in the greater Vermont areas including Burlington, South Burlington, Williston, Essex, Shelburne, and the greater Cittenden County area.
